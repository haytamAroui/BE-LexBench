# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-14

First public release of **be-lexbench** (Belgian Legal Evaluation Suite).

14 tracks across Belgian substantive law (civil, corporate, market practices,
competition, financial compliance, GDPR/digital, employment, insolvency,
administrative, constitutional) and three methodology tracks (citation
integrity, safety calibration, grounded RAG), with anchored 0–4 rubrics and
NL/FR bilingual parity scoring.
