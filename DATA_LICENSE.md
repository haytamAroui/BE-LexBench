The **evaluation data** (item bank, gold answers, scoring splits) is subject to a separate license from the code in this repository.

## Code license

The harness code in this repository (`harness/`, `schema/`, `data/sample/`) is licensed under the GNU GPL v3.0. See [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## Item data license (forthcoming with gated release)

The `be-lexbench` item bank includes items from two source categories:

**Belgian Public Sector Data.** Items derived from Belgian federal or regional open-government materials (statutes, regulations, court decisions published under open data terms) are public domain or subject to standard open-licence terms under Belgian law.

**be-lexbench authored items.** Original items authored by the maintainers are released under terms specified in the full data release agreement. Use requires acceptance of those terms at access-request time.

## Synthetic sample

The items in `data/sample/` are illustrative synthetic content carrying no legal or factual authority. They are released under the same GNU GPL v3.0 terms as the rest of the code in this repository and carry no separate data-license restrictions.
